## Recommendation

Build the **AI Website Agency** as your primary income system. It has the highest chance of producing real revenue within 30 days, the lowest cost, and fits your skill set perfectly. Run the **German digital products** funnel as a secondary system since it's nearly free to set up and piggybacks on deutsch-klinik.

Think of it like this:
- **Primary:** Agency funnel (sells websites to UAE businesses) — active income
- **Secondary:** German medical digital products — passive income
- **Infrastructure:** Both share the same automation tools, email system, and dashboard

Start with the agency. It pays the bills. Then fold in the German stuff as an upsell/parallel track.
