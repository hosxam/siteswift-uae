# Outreach Email Templates

## Strategy

**Golden rule:** Never send mass BCC blasts. Personalize each email. Target 5-10 per day max. Use the lead's name and specific detail about their business.

**Sequence:** Email 1 → 3 days → Email 2 → 5 days → Email 3 → 7 days → Phone call

---

## Template 1: Initial Outreach (Cold Email)

**Subject:** A modern website for [Business Name]?

Hi [Name],

I was looking at [Business Name] and noticed you don't have a website / your site could use an upgrade.

In 2026, most of your customers will search online before visiting. Without a strong site, you're losing them to competitors.

I build modern, fast websites for UAE businesses starting at AED 499/month — no upfront cost, no design skills needed from you. I handle everything.

A few examples:
[Link to portfolio/demo site]

Would you be open to a 10-minute call this week? I can show you what's possible for [Business Name].

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Phone]

## Template 3: Value-add follow-up (7 days after no reply)

**Subject:** 3 things I'd do for [Business Name]'s website

Hi [Name],

One last thought on this. Here's what a new site for [Business Name] would include:

1. A clean, mobile-friendly design that loads in under 2 seconds
2. A way for customers to contact you or book directly
3. Google-friendly structure so you show up in searches for "[Industry/Area]"

That's it. Simple, effective, no fluff.

If you're interested, I can have a draft ready in 48 hours. No commitment needed to see it.

Best,
[Your Name]
