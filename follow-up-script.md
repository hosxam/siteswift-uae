# Follow-Up Script

## After Initial Contact (No Response)

### Day 1 — Send Demo Link

"Hi [Name], I put together a quick concept for what [Business Name]'s website could look like. No need to decide anything yet — just take a look:

[Link to demo draft]

If you like it, we can finalize and go live this week. If not, no worries at all."

---

### Day 3 — Value Reminder

"Hi [Name], just a quick thought. A website doesn't just look professional. It works for you 24/7 — answering questions, taking bookings, bringing in customers while you sleep.

That's what I'd build for [Business Name]. Want to give it a try?"

---

### Day 7 — Last Attempt

"Hey [Name], one last ping. I'd love to get [Business Name] online. Here's my best offer:

1. Free demo site built this week
2. AED 399/month for the first 3 months (instead of AED 499)
3. No commitment, cancel anytime

If you're interested, just say yes. If not, I'll stop reaching out. Either way, all the best."

---

## After Client Signs Up

### Day 1 — Welcome

"Welcome to SiteSwift! 🎉 You're now on the Starter plan. Here's what happens next:

1. Send me your logo (if you have one)
2. Send me 3-5 things you want on your site
3. I'll have your draft ready in 48 hours

Any questions, just reply here."

---

### Day 3 — Draft Ready

"Your draft is ready! Take a look: [Link]

A few things to check:
- Is the information accurate?
- Does the design fit your brand?
- Any changes you'd like?

Reply with your feedback and I'll update it ASAP."

---

### Day 7 — Live Site

"Your site is LIVE! 🚀

[Link to live site]

Here's your next step: Share the link on your Instagram bio and in your WhatsApp status. That's it. Your website is now working for you 24/7.

I'll check in next week with your first monthly report."

---

### Monthly Check-in

"Hi [Name], here's your monthly site report for [Month]:

- Visitors this month: [X]
- Top pages: [page names]
- Contact form submissions: [X]

Anything you'd like updated for next month?"

---

## Upsell Script

"Hi [Name], I noticed your site's been getting good traffic this month. A few ideas to take it further:

1. **Google Business Profile** — I can set this up so you show up on Google Maps/search (AED 199 one-time)
2. **Blog section** — Add 2 articles/month to bring in more search traffic (included in Premium plan)
3. **WhatsApp chat** — Let customers message you directly from the site (AED 0, just ask)

Interested in any of these?"
