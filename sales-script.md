# Sales Script

## Cold Call / WhatsApp Voice Note Script

### Stage 1: Opening (15 seconds)

"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name]. I'm reaching out because I saw [Business Name] on Google Maps / Instagram — great looking business. I noticed you don't have a website yet / your site could use an upgrade."

**Goal:** Quick context, compliment, point out the gap.

---

### Stage 2: Hook (30 seconds)

"In the UAE right now, most of your customers are searching on their phones before they visit. If they can't find you, they find someone else.

I build modern, fast websites for businesses like yours. No upfront cost. Just AED 499/month. I can have a demo ready in 48 hours."

**Goal:** Create urgency, state value, lower barrier (no upfront cost).

---

### Stage 3: Qualification (2 minutes)

Questions to ask:

1. "Do you currently have a website? If yes, what's not working about it?"
2. "How do customers usually find you now?"
3. "Have you thought about getting a website before? What stopped you?"
4. "What are the most important things your website needs to do? Bookings? Contact forms? Show work?"
5. "What's your budget roughly for this?"

**Goal:** Understand their situation, find pain points, qualify them.

---

### Stage 4: Objection Handling

**Objection: "I don't have time for this."**
"I hear you. The good news is you don't need to do anything. I build it, you approve it. 10 minutes of your time total."

**Objection: "I can build it myself on Wix."**
"You could, and Wix is fine. But it'll take you days to learn and build. I can have a better, faster site ready in 48 hours for less than AED 17/day. Your time is worth more than that."

**Objection: "I'm not sure I need a website. Instagram is enough."**
"Instagram is great for reaching people, but it's rented land. You don't control the algorithm. A website is your home on the internet — you own it, you control it. And it helps you show up on Google for people searching for what you do."

**Objection: "It's too expensive."**
"I understand. The Starter plan is AED 499/month — that's less than AED 17/day. For context, one extra customer a month covers it. Most of my clients get 3-5 extra bookings in the first month."

**Objection: "I'll think about it."**
"That makes sense. How about this — let me build you a demo site this week. No charge. If you like it, you subscribe. If not, nothing lost. Deal?"

---

### Stage 5: Close (30 seconds)

"Here's what I propose: Let me build you a demo site this week. It'll cost you nothing to see it. If you love it, you pay AED 499/month going forward. If not, you walk away with a free concept of what your site could look like.

Sound fair?"

**Goal:** Low-risk close. Demo-first approach removes hesitation.

---

### Stage 6: Next Steps

If YES:
"Perfect. Send me your logo if you have one, and tell me 3 things you want customers to know about your business. I'll have something for you in 48 hours."

If "Not right now":
"No problem at all. When would be a good time to check back? I'll note it in my system."

If "No":
"Understood. If things change down the line, feel free to reach out. Have a great day."
